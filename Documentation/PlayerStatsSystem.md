
# **Player Stats Attribute Documentation (Fast Read)**

## **STR – Strength**

*Increases physical damage.*

**Affects:**

* **AD**: +2% per STR

---

## **INT – Intelligence**

*Boosts magic damage.*

**Affects:**

* **AP**: +2% per INT

---

## **AGI – Agility**

*Improves precision and dodge capability.*

**Affects:**

* **Crit Rate**: +0.5% per AGI
* **Evasion**: +0.2% per AGI

**Caps:**

* Crit Rate ≤ **100%**
* Evasion ≤ **75%**

---

## **END – Endurance**

*Your tank stat.*

**Affects:**

* **Max HP**: +5% per END
* **Defense**: +0.5 per END

---

## **WIS – Wisdom**

*Makes you resist CC and magic.*

**Affects:**

* **Defense**: +0.5 per WIS
* **Tenacity**: +0.5% per WIS

**Cap:**

* Tenacity ≤ **80%**

---

# **Derived Stats (Quick Meaning)**

* **AD** = Physical damage
* **AP** = Magic damage
* **HP** = Total health
* **Defense** = Reduces all damage taken
* **Crit Rate** = Chance to crit
* **Crit Damage** = Damage multiplier on crit (items only)
* **Evasion** = Chance to dodge attacks
* **Tenacity** = Reduces CC duration
* **Lethality** = Flat penetration
* **Penetration** = % penetration
* **Lifesteal** = Heals based on damage dealt

---

# **Item-Only Stats**

These **cannot** be increased with attributes:

* Crit Damage
* Lethality
* Penetration
* Lifesteal
* All “ItemXYZ” bonuses

---

# **Ultra-Short Summary**

* **STR → AD**
* **INT → AP**
* **AGI → Crit + Evasion**
* **END → HP + Defense**
* **WIS → Defense + Tenacity**

Everything else comes from items.
